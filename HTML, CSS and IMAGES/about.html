What is Hikikomori?



Hikikomori is a social problem that young people are suffering from all around the world. More than a million people in Japan are suffering just like Hiko (the main character in the game).

However, it is now becoming a global issue. It can affect anyone from teenagers to adults. They lock themselves in their room and isolate themselves from society.

Why create Inwards?



Raise awareness
- The reason I have created this game is because I believe that their isn't enough awareness for the issue.
Help situations
- Inwards isn't meant to be a competitive game, it's a journey and a learning experience. It's important to me that this game will be able to help people who play my game.
Expand Knowledge
- I want to be able to learn and gain knowledge from creating this game. Moreover, getting feedback would be crucial in improving the game further.
